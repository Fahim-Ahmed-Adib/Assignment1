What is Basmati Kacchi?

It was created after Aurangzeb appointed Niza-ul-Mulk as the new ruler of Hyderabad. He took the recipe with him and it spread to other parts of India. His chefs reportedly created almost 50 different recipes that used fish, shrimp, quail, deer and hare meat. This is where kacchi biryani was also perfected.Kacchi Mutton Biryani is a delicious rice dish where tender goat or lamb meat pieces are marinated with lots of fried onions, whole spices, fresh herbs and yoghurt. in kachchi biryani, the marinated meat and Basmati rice are cooked together in a sealed haandi over a slow flame.
